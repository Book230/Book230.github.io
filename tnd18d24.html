Tournaments

In tennis there are 5 different types of events that are played throughout the year. They include 125's, 250's, 500's, 1,000's, and grand slams. These tournaments determine the total amount of points that the winner can earn. At grand slams players can earn between 250 to 2,000 points if they win a tournament .

December 18 - December 24

World Tennis League : December 21- 24 2023

In the World Tennis League , the format consisted of a mixed doubles, mens doubles, womens doubles , mens singles, and womens singles. Additionally, if the score was deuce there would be do ad like usual. There were 4 different teams that would compete against each other. The teams were the eagles, falcons, hawks, and kites. The eagles consisted of Sofia Kenin of the U.S.A, Mirra Andreeva of Russia, Andrey Rublev of Russia, and Daniil Medvedev of Russia. The Falcons consisted of Vera Zvonereva of Russia, Elena Rybakina of Kazahstan, Sorana Cirstea of Romania, Sumit Nagal of India, and Taylor Fritz of the U.S.A. The Hawks consisted of Iga Swiatek of Poland,Hubert Hurkacz of Poland, Casper Ruud of Norway, and Caroline Garcia of France. Lastly, the Kites which consisted of Aryna Sabalenka of Belarus, Paula Badosa of Spain, Grigor Dmitrov of Bulgaria, Llyod Harris of South Africa, and Stefanos Tsistipas of Greece. In the final, it was the Kites versus the Eagles. The Eagles would end up beating the Kites and clinching the title.
