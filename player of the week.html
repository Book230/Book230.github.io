Player of the week

WTA player to 2/26 to 3/3/24: Yue Yuan

Yue Yuan entered the tournament having a remarkable 2024 so far. Yuan had made the semi final in Hobart and the round of 16 in Auckland, but after those results had a slight dip. Coming into the tournament Yuan came into the ATX Open as the number 8 seed and had lost in the 2nd round of qualfying in Doha to Ashlyn Krueger. In the 1st round, she beat Arina Rodionova of Australia 3-6, 6-3, 6-1 who had previously beaten her in Hua Hin. In the second round, she beat Taylor Townsend of the U.S.A. 6-3,7-6. In the quarter final, she beat Yafan Wang of China 6-3, 7-5. In the semi final. she beat Anna Karolina Schmiedlova of Slovakia 6-0,6-3. In the final, she beat fellow Chinese player and 6 seeded Xiyu Wang 6-4,7-6. This allowed her to win her 1st title and to reach the top 50 for the 1st time.
