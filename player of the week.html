Player of the week

WTA player for 5/6 to 5/19/24: Anna Karolina Schmiedlova

Anna Karolina has been on the tour for a while and is considered a veteran. She has achieved a career high of 26 and has won 3 titles on the WTA tour. This season, she had only made one semi-final, which came at the ATX Open. Coming into the Parma Womens Open, she was seeded 4th. In the 1st round, she beat Dalma Galfi of Hungary 6-3,7-6. In the 2nd round, she played Georgia Pedone of Italy 6-3,6-0. In the quarter final, she beat qualifier Jessika Maleckova 6-0,7-5. In the semi-final, she played Jule Niemier of Germany 6-4,6-7,7-5, saving match points in the process. In the final, she beat 8th seeded Mayar Sherif of Egypt 7-5,2-6,6-4. Overall, this weekwas a confidence boast for Anna Karolina and should give her hope going into the French Open.

ATP player for 5/6 to 5/19/24: Alexander Zverev

Zverev had won a title at the Rome Open before and had been playing very good this season. He had made many semi-finals and quarter finals, but hadn't won any titles. In this year's Rome Open, Zverev was seeded 3. In the 1st round, he beat Aleksandar Vukic of Australia 6-0,6-4. In the 2nd round, he beat Luciano Darderdi of Italy 7-6,6-2. In the 3rd round, he beat Nuno Borges of Portugal 6-2,7-5. In thre quarter finals, he beat number 11th seeded Taylor Fritz of the U.S.A. 6-4,6-3. In the semi-finals, he beat number 29th seeded Alejandro Tabilo of Chile 1-6,7-6,6-2. In the final, Zverev beat another Chilean, who was number 21 seeded Nicholas Jarry, 6-4,7-5. As a result of this tournament, Zverev was able to win his first title of the season and hopefully go deep at the French Open.
