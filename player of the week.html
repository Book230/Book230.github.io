Player of the week

WTA player to 12/11 to 12/17/23 : Christina Bucsa

This week , Christina won both the singles and doubles of the Limoges open. Christina won the singles over the home favorite Elsa Jacquemot and the doubles with Yana Sizikova of Russia over Maia Lumsden of the U.K and Oksana Kalashnikova of Georgia. These results allowed her singles ranking to go up by 21 places and to reach a career high of 61. In doubles , her ranking went up 3 places to 64 . This week ultimately was a confidence booster for Christina and gave her confidence going into the 2024 season.

ATP player for 12/11 to 12/17/23 : Jack Draper

Jack Draper or The Power won the title at UTS. In doing so , he beat number 8 ranked Holger Rune of Denmark 16-8 , 16-7,and 17-11. This was Drapers first time playing the event and had no expectations coming into it. Draper won all his matches and had only dropped one set ( 13-15) to the Bublik Enemy or Alexander Bublik. This gave Draper a lot to hope for in the 2024 season , which would start in Australia.
