Player of the week

WTA player to 12/11 to 12/17/23 : Coco Guaff

Coco Guaff had backed up her title at the ASB Classic, where she won it the year before against Rebeka Masarova of Spain. In this years final, she battled versus Elina Svitolina . The match lasted 2 hours and 35 minutes and was the only match of Coco's history at this tournament where she lost a set. Coco lost the 1st set 6-7 although she was up on Svitolina in that set. Coco would end up winning the next 2 sets, 6-3, 6-3. This match was the hardest Guaff every witessed at this tournament, but incredibly. Coco is one out of 4 other woman to win back to back titles at this tournament.

ATP player for 12/11 to 12/17/23: Grigor Dimitrov

Grigor Dmitrov had won his first title in 6 years. His last title was at the Nitto Atp Finals where he beat David Goffin of Belgium 7-5, 4-6,6-3. This tournament win had given a lot of confidence for Grigor going into the Australian Open and into the new season.
