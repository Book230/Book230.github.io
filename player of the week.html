Player of the week

WTA player for 4/29 to 5/5/24: Lois Boisson

Lois who is an upcoming French star, had won her 1st WTA 125 title.she came into this tournament s a wildcard and was a home favorite. In the 1st round, she beat Varvara Gracheva of France 6-4,7-5. In the 2nd round, she beat Solana Sierra of Argentina 6-1,6-0. In the quarter final, she beat number 7th seeded Katie Volynets of the U.S. 5-7,6-2,6-2. In the semi-finals, she beat number 8th seeded Alize Cornet of France, 3-6,6-1,6-4. In the final, she beat Chloe Paquet of France 4-6,7-6,6-3. Overall, this week has given Lois a lot of confidence for the rest of the season.
ATP player for 4/29 to 5/5/24: Andrey Rublev

Andrey came into this tournament as the number 7th seed and had been on a 4 match losing streak. In the 1st round, he beat Facundo Bagnis of Argentina 6-1,6-4. In the second round, he beat number 27th seeded Alejandro Davidovich Fokina of Spain, 7-6, 6-4. In the 3rd round, he beat number 24th seeded Tallon Griekspoor of the Netherlands 6-2, 6-4. In the quarter final, he beat number 2 seeded Carlos Alcaraz of Spain, 4-6, 6-3 ,6-2. In the semi-finals, he beat number 12 seeded Taylor Fritz of Spain 6-4,6-3. In the final, he beat unseeded Felix Auger Aliassime of Spain 4-6,7-5,7-5. Overall, this week helped Andrey gain a lot of confidence and helped to end his losing streak. Additionally, he won hs 2nd ever Masters 1,000 title ever in his career.
