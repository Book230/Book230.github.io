    Player of the week

    WTA player for 3/24 to 3/31/24: Danielle Collins

    Danielle is from the U.S.A. and played college tennis at the University of Virginia and Florida. During her time in college, she has won countless NCAA singles titles. Since then, Danielle has managed to become a successful professional player, making the final of the Australian Open, winning 2 titles, and achieving a career high of 7 in the world in singles. 2024 is Danielleâ€™s last season and will be missed greatly. In Miami, Danielle was unseeded and had to play 7 matches. Her first was against fellow American Bernarda Pera and she won 36,6-1,6-1. In the second round, Danielle beat Anastasia Potapova of Russia who was the 30th seed 6-2,6-2. In the round of 32, she beat Elina Avanesyan of Russia 6-1,6-2. In the round of 16, Danielle beat 19th seeded Sorana Cirstea of Romania 6-3,6-2. In the quarter final, she beat 23rd seeded Caroline Garcia of France 6-3,6-2. In the semi-final, she beat 14th seeded Ekaterina Alexandrova of Russia 6-3,6-2. In the final, Danielle beat the 4th seed Elena Rybakina of Kazakhstan in a hard fought battle, 7-5,6-3. As a result of this, Danielle was able to win her 1st ever 1,000 title and to jump to 22 in the live rankings.

    ATP player for 3/24 to 3/31/24: Grigor Dmitrov

    Grigor had already won Brisbane this year and had a lot of confidence coming into Miami. Grigor was seeded 11th in Miami and had to play 6 matches. His first match was against Alejandro Tabilo of Chile and Grigor beat him 6-7,7-6,6-2. His round of 32 match was against Yannik Hanfmann of Germany and he beat him 6-1,6-0. Grigors round of 16 match was against number 8th seeded Hubert Hurkacz of Poland and he beat him 3-6,6-3,7-6. In the quarter final, Grigor beat top seeded Carlos Alcaraz of Spain 6-2,6-4. In the semi-finals, Grigor beat 4th seeded Alexander Zverev of Germany 6-4,6-7,6-4. In the final, Grigor would lose to red hot Jannik Sinner of Italy 3-6,1-6. Overall,this week was a confidence booster for Grigor and allowed him to re-enter the top 10.
