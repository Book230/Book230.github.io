Player of the week

WTA player to 11/5/23 to 11/11/23 : Iga Swiatek

This week, Iga Swiatek won the year end championship and reclaimed her WTA World number one ranking. She played 5 matches and won all of them. Additionally, she played 5 ten top ten players. In Iga's semi-final match, she got revenge on Aryna Sabalenka and stole the world number one ranking from her.

ATP player for 11/5/23 to 11/11/23 : Novak Djokovic ( Serbia)

During this week, Novak won his 5th title of the year and won the last masters 1,000 of the year. In doing so, Novak played 5 grueling matches, three of which were seeded opponents. Novak's most intense match was against Andrey Rublev in the semi-finals, where he saved match points and beat Andrey Rublev 5-7 , 7-6 , 7-5. This gave Novak a lot of confidence going into the Nitto AtP Finals the following week .
