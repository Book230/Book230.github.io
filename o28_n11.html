Tournaments

In tennis there are 4 different types of events that are played throughout the year. They include 250's, 500's, 1,000's, and grand slams. These tournaments determine the total amount of points that the winner can earn. At grand slams players can earn up to 2,000 points or the lowest at 250 .

October 28- November 11

Wta Finals: Cancun, Mexico: October 29 - November 6

The Wta Finals is a year end tournament that consists of the top 8 performers of the year and is broken into 2 groups. The first group was the Bacalar Group which consisted of Jessica Pegula of the United States, Aryna Sabalenka of Belarus, Maria Sakkari of Greece, and Elena Rybakina of Kazakhstan. The second group was the Chetumal Group and conssted of Iga Swiatek of Poland, Coco Guaff of the United States, Ons Jabeur of Tunisia, and Marketa Vondrousova of the Czech Republic. Jessica Pegula and Aryna Sabalenka were the only two players to advance from the Bacalar Group into the semi finals. From the Chetumal Group, Iga Swiatek and Coco Guaff were the only two who would advance to the semi finals. Guaff would play Pegula and Sabalenka would play Swiatek. Pegula would end up beating Guaff 6-2 6-1 and Swiatek would beat Sabalenka 6-3 6-2. In the final, Swiatek would end up winning the tournament and beat Pegula 6-0 6-1 .

The seeds include: Lorenzo Musetti, Adrian Mannarino, Jan-Lennard Struff, Sebastian Baez, Max Purcell, Sebastian Ofner, Marton Fucsovics, and Miomir Kecmanovic.
