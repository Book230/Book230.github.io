Tournaments

In tennis there are 4 different types of events that are played throughout the year . They include 250's , 500's , 1,000's , and grand slams . These tournaments determine the total amount of points that the winner can earn . At grand slams players can earn up to 2,000 points or the lowest at 250 .

October 28- November 11

Wta Finals, Cancun , Mexico : October 29 - November 6

The Wta Finals is a year end tournament that consists of the top 8 performers of the year and is broken into 2 groups . The first group was the Bacalar Group which consisted of Jessica Pegula of the United States , Aryna Sabalenka of Belarus , Maria Sakkari of Greece , and Elena Rybakina of Kazakhstan . The second group was the Chetumal Group and was conssted of Iga Swiatek of Poland, Coco Guaff of the United States , Ons Jabeur of Tunisia , and Marketa Vondrousova of the Czech Republic . Jessica Pegula and Aryna Sabalenka were the only two players to advance from the Bacalar Group into the semi finals . From the Chetumal Group , Iga Swiatek and Coco Guaff were the only two who would advance to the semi finals . Guaff would play Pegula and Sabalenka would play Swiatek. Pegula would end up beating Guaff 6-2 6-1 and Swiatek would beat Sabalenka 6-3 6-2 . In the final , Swiatek would end up winning the tournament and beat Pegula 6-0 6-1 .

Sofia Open , Sofia , Bulgaria : November 4 - November 11

The Sofia Open was played in Sofia , Bulgaria and is an ATP 250 tournament . The draw is out of 32 and has 9 seeds . The seeds include: Lorenzo Musetti , Adrian Mannarino , Jan-Lennard Struff , Sebastian Baez , Max Purcell, Sebastian Ofner , Marton Fucsovics , and Miomir Kecmanovic . Only the top 4 sedds got byes in this tournament , whch means that they play one less match . Only one seed advanced to the final and that was number 2 seed Adrian Mannarino of France . In the final, he would face Jack Draper of the U.K , who played 5 matches , and beat two seeded players . Mannarino would end up beating Draper 7-6 , 2-6 , 6-3 and would clinch the title .
