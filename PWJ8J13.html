Player of the week

WTA player to 1/8 to 1/13/24 : Emma Navarro

Emma won the Hobart International tournament in Hobart Australia. This was her 1st title on the WTA tour. Emma went to UVA where she played Division 1 tennis and won the national tournament before she won the national title. After this result, she went pro and has rocketed in the rankings over the last year and a half. This week she beat Elise Mertens, Clara Burel, Yue Yuan, Viktorya Tomova, and Magdalena Frech. Throughout the week she had 3 3 set matches and 2 set matches. Overall, this tournament gave her a lot of confience going into the Australian Open and showed that the hard work she had been making payed off.
