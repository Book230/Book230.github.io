Player of the week

WTA player to 11/5/23 to 11/11/23 : Iga Swiatek

This week , Iga Swiatek won the year end championship and reclaimed her WTA World number one ranking . She played 5 matches and won all of them . Additionally, she played 5 ten top ten players . In Iga's semi-final match , she got revenge on Aryna Sabalenka and stole the world number one ranking from her .

ATP player for 11/5/23 to 11/11/23 : Jannik Sinner ( Italy)
