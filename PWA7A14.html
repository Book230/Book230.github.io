Player of the week

WTA player for 4/7 to 4/14/24: Emma Navarro

Emma Navarro has been having a breakthrough season and has been having her best results. These include winning her first WTA title and beating countless top 10 players. This week she was tasked and given the opportunity to play for her country for the 1st time in Billie Jean King Cup. This week, the U.S. played their tie against Belgium at home in Orlando, Florida. Emma helped the U.S. get a 4-0 win over Belgium. She did this by winning her singles match against Hanne Vandewinkel, an up and coming Belgian star.

ATP player for 4/7 to 4/14/24: Stefanos Tsitsipas

Before, this tournament, Stefanos had a somewhat good start to the season making the 4th round of the Australian Open, the semi-finals in Los Cabos, and the quarter final at the Mexican Open. The week before, Stefanos had lost in the 1st round of Miami to Denis Shapovalov, 2-6, 4-6. This week at Monte Carlo, he was seeded 12th. In the 1st round, he beat Laslo Dere of Serbia because of a walkover mid-match. In the next round, he beat Tomas Martin Etcheverry of Argentina 6-1,6-0. In the round of 16, he beat Alexander Zverev 7-5,7-6. In the quarterfinals, he beat Karen Khachanov of Russia 6-4, 6-2. In the semi- he beat red-hot Jannik Sinner of Italy 6-4,3-6,6-4. In the final, he beat Casper Ruud of Norway 6-1,6-4. This amazing result, allowed Stefanos to renter the top 10 and to get his 1st title of the season.
