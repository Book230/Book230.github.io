Welcome to Under Pressure

Within this website, you will learn about the tennis lifestyle and events that are played all throughout the year. Tennis doesn't really have an offseason and the players play tournaments every week within the 52 week year.

WTA

The WTA is the women's tennis organization. The WTA was created in 1973 by the Original 9. The Original 9 included Billie Jean King, Rosie Casals,Nancy Richey,Kerry Melville,Peaches Bartkowicz,Kristy Pigeon, Judy Dalton, Valerie Ziegenfuss and Julie Heldman.These women fought for equal prize money,tournaments, and equal rights for women in the tennis world. If it weren't for them professional women's tennis players today wouldn't be able to play the same amount of tournaments as men,the top players wouldn't be able to earn millions of dollars, and they wouldn't be able to play the sport that they adore at a professional level.

ATP

The ATP is the organization for male tennis players. It was created by Jack Kramer, Donald Dell, and Cliff Drysdale in 1972. This allowed there to a be a computer ranking system for the men that would be updated weekly.
