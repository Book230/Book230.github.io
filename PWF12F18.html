Player of the week

WTA player to 2/12 to 2/18/24: Iga Swiatek

Iga was able to win this tournament for the third consectutive year and was the 1st woman since Serena Williams to win a tournament 3 years in a row. This was only Iga's second tournament of the season because she didn't play any lead up tournaments to the Australian Open. In the final of the Qatar Open, Iga came back from 0-4 down in the 1st set, winning the set 7-6. In the second set, Iga won it 6-2. This was a remarkable triumph for Iga and should allow her to have another dominant season.

ATP player for 2/12 to 2/18/24:Facundo Diaz Acosta

Facundo won his first ever ATP title this week in his home city of Buenos Aires. Throughout the week, Facundo had a lot of support from his family and the Argentian fans. In the final he beat number 3 seeded Nicolas Jarry of Chile who was the favorite. In the prior round, Nicolas beat world number 2 and number 1 seed Carlos Alcaraz. Overall, this tournament gave Facundo a lot of confidence going into the rest of the season and allowed him to reach a current career high of 59.
