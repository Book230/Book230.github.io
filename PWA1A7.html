Player of the week

WTA player for 4/1 to 4/7/24: Camila Osorio Serrano

This week Colombian Camila Osorio Serrano won her second title at home in Bogota. Camila had previously won there in 2021 beating Tamara Zidansek of Slovenia 5-7,6-3,6-4. This year Camila was seeded 6th, but hadn't won that many matches this season. In the 1st round, she beat Canadian qualifier Marina Stakusic 6-4,6-2. In the second round, she beat Anca Todoni of Romania who was another qualifier, 7-6,6-4. In the quarter finals, Camila beat Tatjana Maria of Germany who was on a 14th conscutive streak at the tournament, 1-6,6-3,6-3. In the semi-finals, she beat Sara Errani of Italy, 7-6,6-4. In the final, she beat top seeded Marie Bouzkova of the Czech Republic 6-3,7-6. This win allowed her to victorious at home again and to go up over 20 spots in the live rankings.

ATP player for 4/1 to 4/7/24: Ben Shelton

Coming into Houston, Ben had made countless semi-finals this year and won the ATP 500 tournament in Tokyo at the end of the season. In the 1st round, he played Zizou Bergs of Belgium and beat him 3-6,6-4,6-3. In the quarter finals, he beat fellow American Brandon Nakashima 7-5,7-6. In the semi-finals, he played Tomas Martin Etcheverry of Argentina who was seeded 4th and beat him 6-7,6-4,6-4. In the final, he played fellow American Fracis Tiafoe who had won the tournament the previous year and was seeded third. Shelton would beat Tiafoe,7-5,4-6,6-3. This win would allow Shelton to win his first ever ATP 250 tournament and to become closer to becoming the number one American.
