Player of the week

WTA player to 3/10 to 3/17/24: Elizabetta Cocciaretto

Elizabetta is an up and coming Italian star, that is the 3rd ranked Italian women. She is behind Lucia Bronzetti and Jasmine Paolini. Prior to this week, she hadn't gone that far at any tournament only the second or third round. At the LTP Charleston Open which was a WtA 125 event, she was seeded number 4. In the 1st round, she beat Arina Rodionova of Australia 6-3,6-2. In the 2nd round, she beat Marina Bassols Ribera of Spain 6-0, 6-3. In the quarter final, she beat Mccartney Kessler of the U.S. 7-5,6-3. In the semi finals, she beat Greet Minnen of Belgium 7-6,6-1. In the final, she beat 7th seeded Diana Shnaider of Russia 6-3,6-2 to clinch the title. Overall, this tournament acted as confidence boaster for Elizabetta and should allow her to have a good remainder of the season.

ATP player for 3/10 to 3/17/24: Carlos Alcaraz

Prior to this week, Alcaraz had played in Rio;in his first match,in the 3rd game he twisted his ankle. This forced Alcaraz to take some time off and to recover for Indian Wells. Alcaraz was defending the title in Indian Wells, where he had beaten Daniil Medvedev of Russia 6-3,6-2. In his 1st round, he beat Matteo Arnaldi of Italy 6-7,6-0,6-1. In the 2nd round, he beat number 31st seed Felix Auger Aliassime of Canada 6-2,6-3. In the 4th round, Alcaraz beat Fabian Marozsan who beat him last year in Rome, 6-3,6-3. In the quarter finals, Alcaraz destroyed number 6 seeded Alexander Zverev of Germany,6-3,6-1. In the semi final, Alcaraz broke number 3 seeded Sinner's consecutive 16 match win streak by winning it 1-6,6-3,6-2. In the final, he played Daniil Medvedev yet again and beat him 7-6,6-1.
