Player of the week

WTA player to 11/27/23 to 12/3/23 :Erika Andreeva

Erika Andreeva had made the final of both the singles and the doubles. She won the doubles with Celine Naef of Switzerland. They beat the 2nd seeded Heather Watson and Timea Babos 6-2 6-1. In the singles final Erika lost to 6 seeded Marina Bassols Ribera of Spain. This allowed Erika to have a new career high of 274 and to get back closer to her career high in singles.

ATP player for 11/27/23 to 12/3/23 :Hamad Medjedovic

Hamad won the next gen Atp final beating the number one seed Arthur Fils 3-4, 4-1, 4-2, 3-4 , 4-1. This meant that the two Serbians won the year ending championships , Djokovic winning the Nitto ATp Finals and Hamad winning the Next Gen Atp finals. This gave Hamad a lot of confidence going into the next season.During this tournament , he beat 2 top one hundred players.
