Player of the week

WTA player to 11/12/23 to 11/19/23 : Sara Bejlek ( Czech Republic)

This week was Sara's breakthrough week on the WTA tour. Sara had qualified for both the Australian Open and the French Open, but had lost in the 1st round of both. Additionally , she had won 3 titles on the ITF tour . The ITF tour is below the WTA tour and has the lower ended players . Sara had won one of these titles right before the tournament , which gave her a lot of confidence . At the Colina Open , she played 5 matches and beat 3 seeded opponents . In the 1st round , she beat 6th seeded Anna Bondar of Hungary 5-7, 6-2 , 6-3. In the second round , she beat Ekaterine Gorgodze of Georgia 6-1 , 6-2. In the quarter finals , she beat Panna Udvardy of Hungary 4-6 , 7-6 , 6-4. In the semi finals , she beat Nadia Podoroska of Argentina 6-0, 4-6 , 6-2. In the finals , she beat Diane Parry of France 6-2 , 6-1. As a result , Sara was able to clinch her first ever WTA 125 title and reached her career high of 134 in the world.

ATP player for 11/12/23 to 11/19/23 : Jannik Sinner ( Italy)

Jannik Sinner had never qualified for the Nitto ATP Finals in his whole career , until this point .Jannik was by far having his best season and had won 4 titles . He came into this tournament with a lot of confidence and was playing very fearless . Jannik had easily won his first group stage match vs. Stefanos Tsistipas of Greece 6-4 ,6-4 . In Jannik's next match , he was scheduled to play against Novak Djokovic of Serbia . Novak had a 3-0 record on Jannik prior to this tournament . This record would change after after their match . Jannik would end up upsetting Novak 7-5 , 6-7 , 7-6 and would make it to the final, where he would lose to him .
