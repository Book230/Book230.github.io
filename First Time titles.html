    Jiri Lehecka(Czech Republic) : Adelaide International Open:January 8 - 13 January, 2024

    Ben Shelton has been having his best season so far this year and surprisingly , it has been his first full season . Shelton played for the University of Florida in Gainsville , Florida for 2 seasons for his father Brian Shelton and won the NCAA singles title in 2022 . After having such as a sucessful college career Shelton decided to go pro and his ranking has gradually increased over the last year and a half . Before Shelton won this title , he made the semi-finals at the U.S open and the quarter finals of the Australian open . This had given him a lot of confidence coming into the tournament . During the week of the tournament Shelton had to play 5 intense matches to win the tournament . He best Taro Daniel of Japan in the first round with a score line of 3-6 , 6-4 7-6 . In the second round , he beat Jordan Thompson of Australia with a scoreline of 6-7 , 6-4 , 6-3 . In the quarter finals he best 5th seeded Tommy Paul of the U.s with a scoreline of 7-6 , 6-3 . In the semi's he beat fellow American Marcos Giron 6-7 , 7-6 , 6-4 . In the final he beat Russian Aslan Karatsev with a scoreline of 7-5 , 6-1 . This tournament was a bvery sucessful week for Shelton and helped bring his ranking into the top 15 for he first time.
