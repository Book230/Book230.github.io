Player of the week

WTA player to 12/3 to 12/10/23: Renata Zarazua

Renata is 26 and had reached a career high of 105 as a result of this tournament her former career high was 116 in September of 2021. During this tournament Renata was seeded 2 and had beatwen 3 seeded players including the top seed. Renata beat 6th seeded Miriam Bulgaru of Romainia 6-2, 6-2 in the quarterfinals. In the semis she beat 5th Maria Carle of Argentina 6-1, 2-6, 7-5. In the final, she beat number one seeded Diane Parry of France 7-5, 3-6, 6-4. Overall, this tournament gave her a lot of confidence going into the 2024 season.

ATP player for 12/3 to 12/10/23: Jules Marie

Jules Marie has been on the tour for many years and had primarily played on the challenger tour. Marie is 32 years old and had reached a career high of 228 in 2015. Marie last week had won the M15 Zahra 05a as the top seed in doing so, he played 5 matches and beat 2 seeds. In the final, he beat 863 ranked Elgin Khoeblal of the Netherlands 6-2 6-1. This boosted his ranking by 8 places and resulted in it coming closer to his career high.
